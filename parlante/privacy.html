Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Parlante ("the App") is a native macOS application for editing audio files. This policy describes what data the App uses and where it is stored. We do not collect, transmit, or sell your personal data.

Data processed on your device

The App only works with data on your Mac:

  • Audio and project data — Files you open, edit, or export are read and written only on your machine. No audio or project content is sent to our servers or third parties.
  • Preferences and state — Settings (e.g. last used folders, export defaults, waveform appearance) are stored locally in standard macOS storage (e.g. UserDefaults / Application Support). This data stays on your device.
  • Recent files — Paths to recently opened files may be stored locally for quick access. They are not transmitted elsewhere.

No account, no tracking

The App does not require an account or login. It does not include analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking. We do not collect identifiers, usage statistics, or diagnostics unless you explicitly participate in a feedback or crash-reporting program that we might offer in the future, in which case we would describe that separately.

Permissions

The App may request access to:

  • Files and folders — To open, save, and export audio files in locations you choose.

Such access is used only for the stated features and only on your device.

Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will be revised when we do. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or the App’s handling of data, please contact us through the contact method provided in the App or on the App’s website.
